LEGO STAR WARS NINJAGO MARVEL DC

Ninjago

Long before time had a name, the First Spinjitzu Master used the power of the Golden Weapons to create Ninjago. Trained in the legendary art of Spinjitzu by the wise Master Wu, a group of young ninjas (Kai, Jay, Cole, and Zane, and later Lloyd and Nya) fight with mastery over the elements to defend their land from those who seek to conquer it. Each season focuses on a different one, several, or all of the ninjas and features a different group of varied antagonists.
